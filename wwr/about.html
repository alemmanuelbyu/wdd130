Tidal Wave Adventures

At Tidal Wave Adventures, our mission is to harness the unbridled energy of the river, igniting unforgettable experiences that awaken the thrill-seeker within. We're dedicated to crafting adventures that not only exhilarate, but also inspire a deeper connection with nature and ourselves. Join the tidal wave!

History

Founded in 2014 by a group of fearless river enthusiasts, Tidal Wave Adventures has been charting the untamed waters of the world's most spectacular rivers. From our humble beginnings as a ragtag team of rafting rebels to our current status as a globally-renowned adventure outfitter, our commitment to delivering heart-pounding, soul-stirring experiences has remained unwavering. Today, we proudly continue to push the boundaries of what's possible on the water.
